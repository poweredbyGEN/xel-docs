## What is XEL?

XEL is an open protocol for Living Characters: onchain identities that can be owned, funded, spoken to, preserved, and migrated over time. GEN can publish into XEL and provide services around it, but XEL owns the protocol boundary, public docs, schemas, contracts, and package identity.
